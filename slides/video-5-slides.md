## Taming Information Overload

---

- Emails pile up
- Policy documents arrive
- Meeting notes need review
- Research articles sit unread

# Summarization

---

# Documents You Encounter

- Meeting notes from committees or PLCs
- District policy updates
- Research articles
- Professional development materials

Each valuable. Each time-consuming.

---

# Source-Grounded AI for Summarization

<div class="sketch-box">

Unlike general-purpose AI:
- Works **only** with documents you upload
- Every answer includes **citations**
- You can **verify** the summary matches the source

This matters for accuracy.

</div>

---

![Source-Grounded Summarization](./assets/diagrams/v5-source-grounded-summarization.svg)

---

# Audio Summaries

Some source-grounded tools create **podcast-style summaries**:

- Two AI voices discuss key points
- Natural, engaging format
- Useful while commuting or multitasking
- Can customize the focus

---

<!-- _class: divider -->

# Task Breakdown & Planning

---

# Why Complex Tasks Feel Overwhelming

<div class="callout">

They often lack clear structure.

- Writing a grant proposal
- Preparing for accreditation
- Planning a new curriculum unit

Many steps. Not always obvious where to start.

</div>

---

# AI Can Help Structure

Ask AI to:
- Break a large task into manageable steps
- Suggest a sequence or timeline
- Identify dependencies

This gives you a **starting structure** to adapt.

---

![Task Breakdown](./assets/diagrams/v5-task-breakdown.svg)

---

# The Key Word: Adapt

<div class="sketch-box">

AI does not know:
- Your calendar
- Your workload
- Your school's priorities

**You** adjust timelines, reorder steps, add tasks AI missed, remove what doesn't apply.

AI provides a starting point. You provide professional judgment.

</div>

---

<!-- _class: summary -->

# Key Takeaways

- **Summarization** with source-grounded AI saves time and provides citations
- **Audio summaries** let you learn on the go
- AI can break **complex tasks** into manageable steps
- Always **adapt** AI suggestions to your reality
- AI organizes information; **you** make decisions
